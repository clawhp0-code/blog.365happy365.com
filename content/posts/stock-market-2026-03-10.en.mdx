## Today's Market in One Line

> **"KOSPI explodes +280 points in a single day — the entire market was in celebration mode"**

Today, the Korean stock market had a truly historic day. KOSPI soared more than 280 points in a single day, breaking through the 5,500 level, with cheers from investors echoing through Yeouido's financial district.

---

## Index Trends

| Index | Close | Change | % Change |
|-------|-------|--------|----------|
| KOSPI | 5,532.59 | +280.72 | **+5.35%** |
| KOSDAQ | 1,137.68 | +21.27 | **+1.91%** |

KOSPI saw strong buying pressure from the opening bell, quickly surpassing the 5,500 level. Foreign and institutional investors both turned net buyers, driving an explosive rally led by large-cap stocks. KOSDAQ also responded to KOSPI's strength with a gain of nearly 2%.

---

## Key Issues

- **Tailwinds from the U.S.**: Renewed expectations of a Fed rate cut significantly improved global risk appetite.
- **AI/Semiconductor Momentum Continues**: Domestic semiconductor stocks rallied across the board following NVIDIA's strong earnings.
- **USD/KRW Exchange Rate Stabilizes**: A strengthening won encouraged additional foreign investor inflows.
- **Policy Expectations**: Government signals regarding stock market stimulus measures boosted investor sentiment.

---

## Sector Performance

**Strong Sectors**
- **Semiconductors/IT**: Major semiconductor stocks including Samsung Electronics and SK hynix surged 5-8%
- **Secondary Batteries**: Rallied on renewed expectations of EV demand recovery
- **Financials**: Banks and insurance stocks gained on changing interest rate expectations

**Weak Sectors**
- **Utilities/Telecom**: Relatively overlooked, trading flat
- **Domestic Consumer Goods**: Gains were limited even amid the broad market surge

Today's surge has undeniably elevated short-term overbought concerns. With technical indicators like RSI entering overheated territory, some profit-taking may emerge tomorrow.
