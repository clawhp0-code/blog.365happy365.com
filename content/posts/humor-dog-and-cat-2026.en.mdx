### The Dog's Diary

- 7:00 AM: Wow! My owner woke up!! **SO HAPPY!!**
- 8:00 AM: Wow! My owner fed me!! **SO HAPPY!!**

"My owner does everything for me. **My owner must be God!!**"

### The Cat's Diary

- 7:00 AM: The human woke up. My food bowl is empty. (Displeased)
- 8:00 AM: The human scrambled to open a can and served it. (As expected)
- 2:00 PM: Looking out the window, the humans are scurrying around outside. Pathetic.

**Today's conclusion:**
"That human serves me food, cleans my litter, and performs tricks for me. **I must be God.**"
