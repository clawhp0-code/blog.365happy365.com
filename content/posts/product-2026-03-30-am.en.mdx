## What Is This?

Have you ever stared at the night sky thinking, "Is that Saturn... or just a plane?" 😂 You've always wanted to buy a telescope, but given up every time because of the complicated setup and all the astronomy knowledge you'd need? The **Evolv Scope** is here to completely tear down that wall for you.

The Evolv Scope is a next-generation smart telescope that combines AI auto-alignment with smartphone connectivity. ## Why Is This Special?

The biggest barrier to traditional telescopes was always the **alignment** process. The pain of manually finding three stars and calibrating them one by one... 😤 The Evolv Scope's built-in AI star recognition sensor and GPS module automatically calculate your current location and time, completing **auto-alignment in under 30 seconds**.

Key specs at a glance:

- 🔭 **90mm Refractor Lens** — Clearly observe Saturn's rings, Jupiter's cloud bands, and lunar craters
- 📱 **Dedicated App** — Supports both iOS & Android, with a database of over 80,000 celestial objects
- 🤖 **AI Auto-Tracking** — Compensates for Earth's rotation to automatically keep celestial objects in view
- 📸 **Smartphone Astrophotography** — Supports long-exposure composite shooting via the app, perfect for sharing on social media
- 🔋 **6-Hour Built-in Battery** — Works at outdoor campsites without needing a power outlet

The app UI is incredibly intuitive — it even guides you with arrows showing "there's a star in this direction tonight," so no astronomy knowledge required whatsoever.
