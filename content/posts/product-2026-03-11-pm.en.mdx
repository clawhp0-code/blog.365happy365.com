## What Is This?

Raise your hand if you can't survive a day without coffee. Same here. But what about when you go camping? At a hotel during a business trip? When the office coffee is just terrible? I used to wander around looking for a cafe in those moments. But not anymore. The **Nanopresso Nano** is a **palm-sized espresso machine** that pulls real espresso without a single electrical outlet.

Weighing 336g and measuring 17.5cm long, this water-bottle-sized device produces genuine espresso with crema at 18 bars of pressure.

- - -

## Why Is This Special?

**1. No Electricity Needed — Hand Pump System**
No battery, no outlet required. All you need is hot water. You create 18 bars of pressure by pumping the device by hand, and it's surprisingly easy. Just 20-30 pumps and you've got a real espresso.

**2. Real Espresso with Actual Crema**
Don't underestimate it because it's portable. It produces espresso with beautiful golden crema. You can adjust the taste by tweaking the grind size and tamping pressure, so the more you use it, the more you develop your own recipe.

**3. Easy Disassembly & Simple Maintenance**
All parts come apart for easy water cleaning. Being a compact device, maintenance is hassle-free, so you can keep using it without any annoyance.
