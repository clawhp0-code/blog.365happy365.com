## Netflix Announces InterPositive Acquisition

On March 5, 2026, Netflix announced it would acquire **InterPositive**, an AI filmmaking startup founded by actor Ben Affleck. This is one of the few large-scale acquisitions Netflix has pursued in recent years.

## Acquisition Details

* **Maximum acquisition price: $600 million**
* Structured with an upfront cash payment and performance-based earnout
* InterPositive's owners can earn additional payouts upon achieving certain performance targets

## InterPositive's Technology

InterPositive developed AI-powered filmmaking tools designed for filmmakers:

**Key capabilities:**

* Building AI models based on existing dailies (raw footage)
* Enabling various post-production processes

  * Color Grading
  * Relighting — adjusting lighting in post
  * Adding VFX (Visual Effects)
  * Media Mixing

**Characteristics:**

* Tools built by filmmakers, for filmmakers
* Intuitive workflow that preserves creative control
* Reduces post-production time and maximizes efficiency

## Netflix's Strategy

### InterPositive Team Integration

* All 16 engineers, researchers, and creative team members join Netflix
* Ben Affleck continues as Netflix Senior Adviser

### Expanded Partner Support

* Netflix Creative partners will gain access to InterPositive's technology
* **No plans for commercial sales** — focused on internal and partner use

### A Rare Acquisition Move

Netflix has historically preferred building internally over acquiring external companies. This acquisition signals the importance of AI-powered filmmaking technology.
