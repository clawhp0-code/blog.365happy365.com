## What Makes It Special?

There are exactly three things that set the eVscope apart from regular telescopes.

**1. Auto-Alignment & AI Auto-Search**
Open the smartphone app (Unistellar app), select the celestial object you want, and the telescope **aligns itself automatically.** GPS, gyro sensors, and AI star recognition technology combine to find your target among hundreds of thousands of objects in just seconds. Polar alignment? You don't need that.

**2. Real-Time Enhanced Vision**
A high-sensitivity camera based on a Sony sensor accumulates light in real time, showing you **nebulae, galaxies, and star clusters** that are absolutely invisible to the naked eye. It can even overcome urban light pollution to some degree. The emotion of seeing the Orion Nebula right before your eyes is beyond words.

**3. Citizen Science Project Participation**
In collaboration with NASA, SETI, and others, you can **contribute your observation data to real science projects** like asteroid observation and comet tracking. It's like becoming a real astronomer! Hobby and contribution at the same time!
