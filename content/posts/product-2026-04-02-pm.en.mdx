## What Is This?

Have you ever stared at the night sky thinking, "Is that Orion? Or just a plane?" and spaced out? Not just me, right? 😂

The **Pocket Star Map** is an ultra-compact star chart that folds down to business-card size. The entire northern hemisphere's constellations are precisely printed on high-quality waterproof-coated paper or thin aluminum sheet, and thanks to a special folding method, it doesn't look out of place in a card wallet. Unfold it, and a full star chart — twice the size of A4 paper — reveals itself with a satisfying *flourish*.

As of 2026, the camping and glamping population has exploded, quietly growing the "night sky appreciation item" category. The Pocket Star Map is right at the center of that, quietly becoming a hit.

## Why Is This Special?

You might say there are smartphone apps for that — and you're right. But apps **drain the battery** and the screen brightness actually interferes with your eyes adapting to the dark. In astronomy clubs, the "red lantern + paper map" combination is still considered the standard approach.

Key features of the Pocket Star Map:

- 🌟 **Monthly rotating disk included**: Rotate the center disc to highlight only the constellations visible for the current month and time of day.
- 💧 **Waterproof coating**: No problem even in dewy early-morning campsites.
- 🗺️ **Latitude-specific version**: A version specifically calibrated for Korea (37°N latitude) is available for higher accuracy.
- ✏️ **Memo space on the back**: You can write your own observation log, which satisfies the collector's instinct.

The combination of analog aesthetic + practicality is perfectly on-trend right now, don't you think?
