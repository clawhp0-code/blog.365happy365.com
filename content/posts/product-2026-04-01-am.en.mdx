## Why Is This Special?

There are a few decisive differences from regular star lights.

**① Real-time constellation mapping**
Connect via Bluetooth to the app (supports both iOS and Android), and it uses your current GPS location and date to recreate the actual sky. Point the app at a star and it tells you the name. Genuinely great as an intro to astronomy.

**② Laser + LED dual light source**
Not just simple LED point lights — the combination of laser and LED expresses differences in star brightness realistically. First-magnitude stars bright, third-magnitude stars faint. This detail dramatically increases immersion.

**③ Sleep timer + built-in white noise**
Turn on rain sounds, ocean waves, or forest sounds via the built-in speaker, set a 30-minute timer, and you just... pass out. This is why so many reviews mention noticeably improved sleep quality.

**④ Color & rotation customization**
Star color can be changed to white, blue, aurora green, and more. Turn on slow-rotation mode and the stars appear to actually move. Once you're watching this, you lose track of time.
