## Making "Going Viral" a Profession

In the 2020s, the rules of marketing changed.

Advertising moved from TV to smartphones, and on smartphones, there was only one stage that mattered.

**TikTok.**

A single 15-second video could rack up millions of views overnight, and an unknown product could sell out in a "TikTok viral" moment. Brands were caught off guard. The old marketing playbook no longer worked.

That is when a young man in Las Vegas had a thought.

*"What if I could organize this chaos and sell it?"*

His name was **Abe Ledezma**. The company he created is **Viralux**.

## Who Is Abe Ledezma

Abe Ledezma is a Las Vegas-based marketer and entrepreneur.

His career did not start with an impressive resume. He worked at Cashman Photo Enterprises, gaining experience in events, photography, and the media industry. Then, as TikTok was growing explosively, he spotted a new opportunity.

Specifically, **TikTok Shop**.

TikTok Shop is a commerce feature that goes beyond a simple video platform, allowing products to be sold directly. When viewers see a product they like in a video, they can hit the buy button right then and there. It merged video and shopping into one.

Ledezma found the gap in this market.

> *"Brands want to use TikTok Shop. But they do not know how. We can do it for them."*

He assembled a team and founded **Viralux Media**.

## What Viralux Sells: "The Formula for Going Viral"

Viralux Media's services to brands can be summarized in three areas.

**1. TikTok Shop Strategy**
Which product, in what format, at what timing to sell on TikTok. They analyze the platform's algorithm and consumer psychology to design customized strategies for each brand.

**2. Creator Partnerships**
They maintain a network of thousands of TikTok creators and connect them with brands. Rather than simple ads, products are naturally woven into creators' organic content. This is the key to "real virality."

**3. End-to-End Management**
From content planning to filming support, upload timing, and performance analytics, they handle all aspects of TikTok Shop operations. From the brand's perspective, they do not need to know TikTok. Viralux handles everything.

### Key Features of Viralux.ai

**Max Watch Time Optimization**
Beyond simply creating videos, the AI automatically optimizes the first 3-second hook, editing rhythm, and subtitle style so viewers do not skip.

**Short-Form Dedicated Format**
Optimized for the 9:16 vertical aspect ratio. Ready to upload directly to TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

**Text-to-Video**
Input desired content as text to auto-generate videos up to 8 seconds long. Applicable for product introductions, review videos, ad clips, and more.

**A/B Testing Support**
Generate multiple video versions to compare performance. See which hook is more effective or which subtitle style drives higher conversion rates through data.

A free tier is available, with paid plans offering more generation credits and features.

## ViraLux Studio: Translating Into 108 Languages

There is another tool that uses the Viralux name.

**ViraLux Studio (viralux.xyz)** is an AI video translation and dubbing platform. It automatically translates existing videos into over 108 languages and applies AI voice dubbing.

Upload videos from YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook, and it will:

* Automatically detect the language
* Generate SRT/VTT subtitle files
* Apply AI voice dubbing
* Extract MP3/WAV audio

With a user rating of **4.9 out of 5 (150 reviews)**, it shows high satisfaction.

This is especially interesting for creators. If you can simultaneously create English, Spanish, and Japanese versions from a single video, the barrier to entering the global market drops significantly.

## How TikTok Shop Changed the Commerce Landscape

![Mobile Shopping](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/36/Two_women_looking_at_a_cell_phone.jpg/400px-Two_women_looking_at_a_cell_phone.jpg)

*TikTok is evolving beyond a video platform into a commerce platform*

To understand Viralux's growth, you need to understand the market TikTok Shop opened up.

Traditional e-commerce worked like this: consumers search for what they need, compare products, and purchase. It is intentional buying.

TikTok Shop is different. Consumers watch entertaining videos, develop interest in a product without realizing it, and make impulse purchases. This approach, called **Discovery Commerce**, is proving especially powerful among younger generations.

Monthly transaction volume on U.S. TikTok Shop grew to billions of dollars as of 2024. In China, it has already grown large enough to threaten Alibaba's commerce business. TikTok Shop's influence is rapidly expanding in other markets as well.

Viralux had its surfboard ready before the wave arrived.
