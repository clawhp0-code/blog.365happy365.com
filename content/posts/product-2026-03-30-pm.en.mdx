## What Is This?

Would you believe that something small enough to fit in the side pocket of your commuter bag can project a **screen up to 120 inches wide** when aimed at a wall? That's the story of the **ultra-compact portable laser projector** that's been setting the tech community on fire in 2026.

Just 3–4 years ago, "mini projector" meant blurry image quality, a battery that drained fast, and honestly, something you carried around just for the look of it. But 2026 models are in a completely different league. The combination of laser light sources and AI auto-correction technology has turned these into devices you can actually use for real.

---

## Why Is This Special?

The core of this new generation of mini projectors comes down to three things:

**① Dramatically Improved Image Quality with Laser Light Source**
Unlike conventional LED-based projectors, laser light sources offer far superior color accuracy. You'll find yourself instinctively saying "Wow, this is actually sharp" when you use one. With brightness of 700+ ANSI lumens, it's quite watchable even without a completely dark room.

**② AI Auto-Correction**
Even if you place it at an angle, the camera recognizes the screen and automatically corrects it into a proper rectangle. Even in a camping tent on an uneven surface, it adjusts on its own. Initial setup takes less than 10 seconds.

**③ Built-in Battery + Android OS**
You can run YouTube and Netflix natively without connecting a separate device, and a full charge provides about 2–2.5 hours of playback. Enough to comfortably get through an entire movie.

Weight is a mere **~450g** — roughly the same as two cans of soda.
