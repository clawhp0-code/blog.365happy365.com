9 AM start? Free spirits clock in at 9:15.

---

### 2. The longer the meeting, the better...

As long as you're not the one presenting.

---

### 3. The printer always breaks when you're in a rush

This is a law of physics. Even Einstein acknowledged it.

### 5. There's no leaving at 4 PM on Friday

A meeting pops up, the weather's nice, the boss drops by...

When payday comes, you heroically show up to work again.

"What should we eat today?" = humanity's greatest unsolved problem
