### At an Interview Room of a Small Company

A job interview was underway at a small IT company.
In the tense interview room, the interviewer (a department manager) fixed a sharp gaze on the last remaining candidate and fired a question.

**Manager:** "Our company works quite a lot of overtime. We often have all-nighters too. Are you confident in your stamina?"

**Candidate:** "Yes! No problem at all! In college, I stayed up every night playing games, so I'm very confident about pulling all-nighters!"

The manager smiled with satisfaction and asked one more question.

**Manager:** "Good. Then lastly... if you join our company, what is your most powerful weapon? How can you contribute to the company?"

Without a moment's hesitation, the candidate's eyes sparkled as he answered.

.
.
.

**Candidate:** "My most powerful weapon is... **'youth'**! I will dedicate my youth to making this company shine!"

The manager was so moved that he stood up and applauded.
"You're hired!! Start tomorrow!!"

---

### The Next Morning

The next morning at 9:00 AM.
It was the new hire's first day of work.
But past 9:30, his desk was still empty.

It was nearly 10:00 when the new hire came rushing in, out of breath.

**Manager:** (incredulous) "What? Being late on your very first day?! Where did all that boldness from yesterday go?"

The new hire, gasping for air, answered confidently.

.
.
.

**New Hire:** "Manager... Didn't I tell you that my most powerful weapon is **'youth'**?"

**Manager:** "...And?"

**New Hire:** **"I'm young, so I need way too much sleep!!!"**

Manager: (clutches the back of his neck)
