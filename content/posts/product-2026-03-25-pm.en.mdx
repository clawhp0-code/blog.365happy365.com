## What Makes It Special?

The difference between a traditional telescope and the eVscope is like **paper map vs. Google Maps**. Let me break down the key features.

### Auto-Alignment (Autonomous Field Detection)
The complicated process of manually finding and aiming at stars? Not needed. Built-in sensors and AI instantly recognize star patterns in the sky and **automatically align to the target celestial object within 3 minutes**. Saturn's rings, the Andromeda Galaxy — one tap in the app and it finds them automatically.

### Smartphone Live View
Instead of pressing your eye to an eyepiece, you watch **real-time high-quality video on your smartphone screen**. Multiple people can enjoy the view together, and screenshots and video recording are instant.

### Light Pollution Defeating Technology
Downtown is perfectly fine. The 'Enhanced Vision' technology accumulates and composites long-exposure images in real time to vividly display **nebulae and galaxies that are absolutely invisible to the naked eye**. See the Orion Nebula from a riverside park in a major city? Yes, it actually works.

### Citizen Science Participation
In collaboration with the SETI Institute, your telescope data **automatically contributes to real asteroid tracking and comet observation research**. It feels like becoming an astronomer yourself!
