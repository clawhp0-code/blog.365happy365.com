## What Is This?

When you drag your tired body home after work, lie down in bed, and look up at the ceiling... it's just a white ceiling. But what if that ceiling were filled with thousands of stars and the Milky Way, just like the real night sky?

A **Smart Home Planetarium** is a compact celestial projector that precisely projects constellations, planets, and the Milky Way onto your ceiling and walls anywhere indoors, all based on real astronomical data. Between 2025 and 2026, these devices evolved rapidly, moving beyond simple mood lighting to a level where app integration can recreate **the actual sky visible from your location tonight**.

---

## What Makes It Special?

This is on a completely different level from conventional starlight mood lamps. Let me break down the highlights!

**1. Real-Time GPS-Linked Astronomical Data**
Connect to your smartphone app via Bluetooth, and it **recreates the actual night sky** based on your current location, date, and time. Curiosities like "Where is Orion tonight?" get answered right from your bed.

**2. 8,000+ Stars + Nebulae/Galaxy Rendering**
These aren't just simple light dots — they reflect each star's brightness class and color temperature. Red Betelgeuse, blue Rigel... each star has its own color, making the viewing experience truly rich.

**3. Sleep Timer + Sunrise Mode**
In sleep mode, the starlight gradually dims and turns off. In the morning, it simulates a sunrise to gently wake you up — a built-in alarm clock feature.

**4. 360-Degree Rotation + Auto Tracking**
Stars slowly move at the pace of Earth's rotation. Watching it, you genuinely feel like you're lying outside under the open sky.
