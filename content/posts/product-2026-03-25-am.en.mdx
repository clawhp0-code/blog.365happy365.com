## What Is This?

Haven't we all been in that situation abroad where a restaurant server is speaking rapidly and you can't understand a single word, so you just nod along?

I had a close call myself on a business trip to Japan last year — the client was passionately explaining something and I just kept saying "ah, yes, yes" and nearly caused a major issue later.

But here's the thing — the **Timekettle WT2 Edge** completely solves that problem. Just pop them in your ears like regular earbuds and the other person's speech is **translated into your language in real time**. That scene from sci-fi movies? In 2026, it's reality.

---

## What Makes It Special?

The WT2 Edge has a decisive edge over conventional translators.

**Innovative Simultaneous Interpretation**
Two people each wear one earbud, and the moment either speaks, the translated audio plays in the other person's ear. No need to stare at a smartphone screen — you can make eye contact and have a natural conversation. This is the real game changer.

**0.5-Second Speed**
From recognition to translated output in about 0.5 seconds. The flow of conversation never breaks. It's a completely different dimension from the days of opening a translation app, pressing the mic button, and waiting.

**40 Languages, 93 Accents Supported**
English, Japanese, Chinese, of course, but also Arabic, Spanish, Vietnamese, and more. Handles Southeast Asian travel or European business trips with ease.

**Offline Mode Included**
Basic translation works even without Wi-Fi or data. No worries in remote villages or situations where data roaming isn't available.
