While older star lights were basically at the level of scattering dots of light, today's foldable projectors are on a completely different level.

- **88 real constellation patterns built in** — Enjoy and learn about seasonal constellations like Orion, the Big Dipper, and Scorpius.
- **RGB full-color support** — Switch between sky blue, purple nebula, orange sunset, and more to match your mood.
- **Auto-off timer** — Set it for 1 or 2 hours to automatically turn off before you fall asleep, with no electricity worries!
- **Foldable ultra-light design** — Unfold to adjust the projection angle, fold down to under 200g for easy storage and portability.
