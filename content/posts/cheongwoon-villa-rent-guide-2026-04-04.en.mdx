**Cheongwun-dong (청운동)** is a quiet hillside neighborhood in Jongno-gu, Seoul — tucked beneath Inwangsan Mountain, west of Gyeongbokgung Palace, and right next to the Cheong Wa Dae (Blue House) grounds, which have been open to the public since 2022.

It's a neighborhood that doesn't show up much on mainstream apps. Listings are sparse. But for those who want a calm, scenic spot within reach of central Seoul, it's worth knowing how to search.
