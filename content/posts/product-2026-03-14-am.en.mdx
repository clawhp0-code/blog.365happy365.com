## What Is This?

You've looked up at the night sky and wondered, "What star is that?" right? But when you actually buy a telescope — **give up trying to focus**, give up trying to find stars, and it ends up buried in the closet. That sad telescope history... the Equinox 3 puts a definitive end to it.

Unistellar's **Equinox 3** is a smart telescope equipped with AI and auto-tracking technology. Open the app and say "show me Saturn," and the telescope **rotates on its own to find it automatically**. Seriously.

## Why Is This Special?

The difference between a traditional telescope and the Equinox 3 is like **paper maps vs. Google Maps**.

**4 Key Features**

1. **AI Auto-Alignment (Enhanced Vision)**: It clearly captures planets, nebulae, and galaxies even through urban light pollution. The sensor accumulates light to display things invisible to the naked eye on screen.

2. **Full Smartphone App Integration**: Select an observation target in the iOS/Android app and you're done. The telescope automatically aims and tracks.

3. **Citizen Science Participation**: Connect to the Unistellar network and you can provide asteroid observation data to NASA in real time alongside users worldwide. A telescope that contributes to real science!

4. **Compact Size**: Weighing about 5kg, it fits in a travel bag for camping or suburban outings.

With a 77mm aperture, F/4 optical system, it clearly captures Saturn's rings, Jupiter's atmospheric bands, and the Orion Nebula. It's the upgrade from "I like stars" to **"I can show you the stars."**
