## Why Is This Special?

Do you know what the biggest problem with traditional telescopes has always been? **Alignment is too hard.** You spend good money, can't figure out the calibration, and it ends up collecting dust in the closet.

The Stellina 2 completely flipped this problem.

- 📡 **Auto-Alignment**: Power it on and connect the app — GPS and internal sensors automatically calculate the sky coordinates. Setup time? 3 minutes is enough.
- 🌈 **Stacking Technology**: Multiple photos are composited in real time, revealing even the colors of nebulae in crisp detail. The moment the red glow of the Orion Nebula — invisible to the naked eye — appears on your phone screen... actual goosebumps.
- 📱 **Multi-device sharing**: Connect via Wi-Fi and multiple family members or friends can enjoy the same view simultaneously. Use it at a campsite and you're guaranteed instant popularity.
- ⚡ **IP54 dust and water resistance**: No worries using it outdoors.

Another unique point: you don't need to put your eye to the lens. It's 100% digital viewer format — comfortable for glasses wearers and kids alike.
