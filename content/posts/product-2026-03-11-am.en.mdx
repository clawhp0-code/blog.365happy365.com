## What Is This?

Don't you make that resolution every New Year — "This year, I'm going to take care of my health"? You sign up for the gym, but you can't properly track your body's condition, and the whole thing fizzles out in three days. That pattern can change now. The **Garmin Vivoactive 6** isn't just a watch. It's a **health partner on your wrist** that collects your 24-hour health data and gives you personalized coaching advice.

- - -

## Why Is This Special?

Let me highlight three key differences that set the Garmin Vivoactive 6 apart from other smartwatches.

**1. Body Battery Energy Tracking**
It shows your energy level throughout the day as a real-time score from 0 to 100. It tells you data-driven insights like "now is a good time to exercise" or "you should rest today." It's excellent for preventing overtraining.

**2. Sleep Score + Sleep Coaching**
It doesn't just measure sleep duration. It analyzes your REM sleep, deep sleep, and light sleep ratios, assigns a "sleep score," and suggests specific improvements. You can now see firsthand through data how accumulated sleep deprivation wrecks your body.

**3. Blood Oxygen + Heart Rate Variability (HRV) Monitoring**
It continuously measures blood oxygen saturation (SpO2) and heart rate variability. HRV is a key indicator for understanding stress and recovery status, and being able to track it 24/7 from your wrist is a real game-changer.
