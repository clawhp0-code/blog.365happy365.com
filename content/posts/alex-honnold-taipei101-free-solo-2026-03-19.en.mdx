## 🏔️ A Historic Moment: Taipei 101 Free Solo Success

The world's greatest free solo climber **Alex Honnold** successfully ascended Taiwan's Taipei 101 without ropes through Netflix's live event **"Skyscraper Live"**. This is the highest free solo record for an urban building.

## ⏱️ Ascent Record

* **Building**: Taipei 101 (Height: 1,667 feet / approx. 508m)
* **Ascent Time**: 1 hour 31 minutes
* **Start Time**: 9:00 AM local time
* **Summit Time**: 10:31 AM
* **Record**: Largest urban free solo in history

## 🧗 Who Is Alex Honnold?

**Definition of Free Solo:**

* Climbing vertical cliffs or buildings with bare hands, without ropes or protective equipment
* An extreme sport requiring intense focus, physical ability, and psychological fortitude

**Alex Honnold's Career:**

* The world's most famous free solo climber
* Subject of numerous documentaries and books
* Completed the most difficult climbs at Yosemite's El Capitan, Half Dome, and more
* This urban building free solo represents a new frontier

## 🌆 The Significance of Taipei 101

**Features of Taipei 101:**

* A 101-story supertall building located in Taipei, Taiwan
* One of the tallest buildings in the world
* Famous for its unique architectural design
* Extreme challenges including strong winds, height, and urban environment

**Difficulties of the Free Solo Challenge:**

* Unpredictable factors in urban environments (wind, temperature, humidity)
* Extreme height and psychological burden
* Inability to respond to emergencies
