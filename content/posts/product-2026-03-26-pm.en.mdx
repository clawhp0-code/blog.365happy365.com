## What Makes It Special?

There are exactly three reasons this kit stands apart from other paper models.

**First, officially licensed precision.** Based on real design data from the NASA archives, the exterior proportions, colors, and logo placement are printed identically to the real thing. For detail obsessives, it's pure paradise.

**Second, special coated paper.** Not ordinary paper — these are printed on 160g glossy cardstock with UV coating, so the colors won't fade and tearing is nearly impossible during assembly. The finished product's surface is sturdy enough to last years as a desk display piece.

**Third, three difficulty levels to choose from.** From the beginner-friendly 'Easy Cut' version to the enthusiast 'Expert' version, even paper craft newcomers can feel a sense of accomplishment. At the highest difficulty, the parts count goes up to 420 pieces, firing up that competitive spirit!
