## Note-Taking AI Is Already Outdated

Otter.ai, Fireflies, Clova Note... AI tools for meeting transcription and summarization are already a dime a dozen. But do you know what they all have in common?

**They are all 'after-the-fact' tools.**

You only get your summary after the meeting ends, and your action items only appear once it's over. Meanwhile, you have to find the information you need *during* the meeting on your own.

**Proactor AI is different.** This service's catchphrase is "the world's first proactive AI agent" — it acts *before* you even ask.

---

## What Is Proactor AI?

![AI meeting assistant](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1677442135703-1787eea5ce01?w=900&auto=format&fit=crop)

Officially launched in June 2025 in Wyoming, USA, it is an **AI meeting assistant SaaS service**. In simple terms, think of it as "a digital assistant that joins you in the meeting room, taking notes, searching for information, and organizing everything while you speak."

If existing tools are like a **recorder**, Proactor is more like a **colleague who thinks in real-time**.

---

## 5 Core Features

### 1. Real-Time Insight Stream

While discussions are happening, it **continuously updates summaries, risk factors, and follow-up suggestions**. It's like having an analyst sitting next to you, whispering "this part is important" or "that client raised a similar objection last time."

### 2. Automatic To-Do Lists

Statements like "I'll send you a draft by next week" — **action items implicitly promised during conversation** — are automatically detected and extracted into a to-do list. By the time the meeting ends, your To-Dos are already organized.

### 3. Meeting Wiki

After each session, an overview, conclusion, key takeaways, and to-dos are automatically generated as a structured document. The quality is good enough to use directly for reporting.

### 4. Potor Chat Assistant

You can ask the built-in AI chatbot **Potor** questions based on previous meeting records. Ask "What was the objection the client raised last month?" and it will search through past transcripts and give you the exact answer.

### 5. Real-Time Research

Competitors, data, and terminology mentioned during the meeting are **quietly searched in the background**, providing relevant information. No need to switch tabs mid-meeting to Google something.
