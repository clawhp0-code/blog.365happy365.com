## What Is This?

You've played with LEGO at least once as a kid, right? But this isn't just any LEGO. This is the **LEGO Technic Hubble Space Telescope (42369)** set. Since its release in late 2025, it's been hailed as "the kidult item of the year" among space enthusiasts and LEGO fans alike.

It recreates the actual Hubble Space Telescope at 1:48 scale, and the completed build measures an impressive **48cm**. Put it on your desk and from that day forward, your room becomes a NASA research lab. I'm not joking.

---

## Why Is This Special?

First of all, the number **2,659 pieces** alone is no joke. But what matters more than the number is the detail.

- **True-to-scale reproduction**: Solar panels, primary mirror, and antenna faithfully reflect the real structure
- **Functional mechanisms**: Solar panels actually extend and fold, and the telescope body rotates 360 degrees
- **Official NASA collaboration**: Includes an authentic plate with the NASA logo — perfect for gifts
- **Hubble history booklet**: The instruction manual includes a mini booklet with Hubble's history and the space photographs it captured

The build difficulty is "challenging but not impossible." Average assembly time is about **8-12 hours**. You'll need to commit an entire weekend day, but the satisfaction is beyond words. There's a reason people post reviews saying they nearly teared up placing the final piece.

Thanks to Technic's signature **pin + beam construction**, the completed model is extremely durable, so no worries about it falling apart on display!
