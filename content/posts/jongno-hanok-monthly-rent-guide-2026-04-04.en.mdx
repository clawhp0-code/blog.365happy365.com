If you want to live in a traditional Korean hanok (한옥) in Seoul, **Jongno-gu** is where you start. Bukchon, Seochon, and Ikseon-dong — the three main hanok neighborhoods in the city — are all located here.

The problem: hanok rentals rarely show up on mainstream real estate apps. You need to know where to look. This guide covers every useful search channel, with direct links.

## Where to Find Hanok Rental Listings

### 1. Seochon Spring — Hanok Specialist Agency

This agency focuses exclusively on hanok properties in Seochon and Bukchon — sales, jeonse (long-term deposit lease), and monthly rentals. Their listing page is the most targeted resource available for these areas.

- **Current listings**: [Seochon Spring — Browse Listings](https://www.seochonspring.com/%EB%A7%A4%EB%AC%BC%EB%B3%B4%EA%B8%B0)
- **Hanok-only page**: [Seochon Spring — Hanok](https://www.seochonspring.com/%ED%95%9C%EC%98%A5)

---

### 2. Seoul Hanok Portal — Official City Resource

Operated by the Seoul Metropolitan Government. Covers hanok registration data, city support programs, and public hanok rental information alongside cultural and accommodation listings.

- **Seoul Hanok Portal**: [hanok.seoul.go.kr](https://hanok.seoul.go.kr/)

---

### 3. Balpoom Top Real Estate — Hanok Listing Category

A real estate site with a dedicated hanok listings category. Good for browsing available hanok properties across Seoul including the Jongno area.

- **Hanok listings**: [Top Real Estate — Hanok](https://top4949.com/boodong_list.php?cate=medo&sc=13)

---

### 4. Luxury N House — Premium & Hanok Properties

Handles upscale residential properties including hanok. Useful for larger or higher-end hanok units.

- **Hanok search**: [Luxury N House](http://m.luxurynhouse.com/selling/selling_list.asp?d_kind_01_main=13)

---

### 5. Daangn Market Real Estate — Direct Listings

Korea's local classifieds app. Hanok rentals occasionally appear here as direct listings from owners, which can mean lower or no agency fees.

- **Jongno 3-ga listings**: [Daangn — Jongno 3-ga](https://www.daangn.com/kr/realty/?in=%EC%A2%85%EB%A1%9C3%EA%B0%80-6313)
- **Jongno 5-ga listings**: [Daangn — Jongno 5-ga](https://www.daangn.com/kr/realty/?in=%EC%A2%85%EB%A1%9C5%EA%B0%80-6315)

---

### 6. Jongno-gu Official Real Estate Portal

Maintained by Jongno-gu district office. Useful for finding registered local real estate agencies that handle hanok properties.

- **Jongno Real Estate Portal**: [land.jongno.go.kr](http://land.jongno.go.kr/land/)

## Checklist Before Signing a Hanok Lease

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                   Hanok Rental Checklist                              │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                      │
│  🔲 Winter insulation — older hanok can be drafty; check windows     │
│  🔲 Bathroom location — some traditional hanok have outdoor toilets  │
│  🔲 Heating system — ondol (floor heating) and gas connection        │
│  🔲 Heritage zone restrictions — renovation or decor may be limited  │
│  🔲 Parking — Bukchon and Seochon have almost no parking             │
│  🔲 Tourist foot traffic — some Bukchon lanes are on tour routes     │
│                                                                      │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

---

Hanok rentals reward persistence. Bookmark the sites above, check them regularly, and consider visiting Seochon or Bukchon in person — some of the best listings are posted only on agency windows or passed by word of mouth.
