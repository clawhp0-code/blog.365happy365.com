## What Is This?

What if you had a Saturn sitting on your desk? 🪐

The "Saturn BT-01" is a Bluetooth speaker that recreates Saturn's ring structure and surface texture down to 0.1mm precision, using NASA photos as reference. The spherical speaker body — about 15cm in diameter — becomes Saturn's body, and an acrylic ring surrounds it, tilted like actual rings. Turn it on and hidden RGB LEDs at the base of the ring glow softly, creating an atmosphere as if a real planet is floating in space.

If your first reaction upon opening the box was "Wait, is this actually a speaker?" — you've already fallen under this thing's spell.

## Why Is This Special?

It'd be a shame to only talk about the design. The sound is fairly serious too.

- **Driver**: Dual 40mm full-range drivers provide more punch in the mids and bass than you'd expect from a mini speaker. None of the tinny sound typical of small speakers.
- **Battery**: 2,000mAh built-in battery gives about 8 hours of continuous playback — plenty for an all-day desk session.
- **Connectivity**: Bluetooth 5.3 means almost no dropouts. Stable even at 10m distance.
- **Lighting modes**: Ring LED supports three modes — solid color, breathing, and music-reactive. In music-reactive mode, the light pulses with the beat, adding a visual dimension.
- **Materials**: ABS + silicone-coated body, semi-transparent acrylic ring — it looks premium.

But more than anything, the Saturn BT-01's real superpower is its **potential as a gift**. A friend who loves space, a professor in science, parents who are astronomy enthusiasts... whoever you give it to, you'll get a "Where did you even find this?!" reaction.
