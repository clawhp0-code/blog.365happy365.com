## Privacy Policy - walkmylife25

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

### 1. Information Collection

This app (walkmylife25) does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

### 2. TikTok Account Access

The app only accesses the authenticated TikTok account to publish video content on behalf of the account owner. Authentication is handled through TikTok's official OAuth process.

### 3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

### 4. Data Storage

No personal information is stored on external servers. All authentication tokens are stored locally on the app owner's device.

### 5. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

### 6. Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact: walkmylife25@gmail.com
