## What Is This?

Honestly, I was skeptical at first. "AI built into glasses? Isn't that just sunglasses with Bluetooth earbuds?" That was my initial reaction. But once I actually tried them on... I completely changed my mind.

As of 2026, the AI smart glasses market is exploding. Starting with Meta's Ray-Ban Smart Glasses, the second-generation products from multiple brands released earlier this year have gone far beyond **simple camera glasses** — they've leveled up to genuinely being "an AI assistant on your eyes." And at just around 45g, you can barely feel the difference from wearing regular glasses.

---

## Why Are These Special?

The most stunning part is the **combination of real-time visual recognition + voice AI**. Here are some examples:

- 🍽️ **Look at a restaurant menu** → Automatic translation + calorie information whispered in your ear
- 🗺️ **Walking down an unfamiliar street** → "There's a convenience store 100m to your left" — natural navigation
- 👤 **See a business card** → Instantly save the contact & get a summary of the person's LinkedIn profile
- 📸 **Take a photo** → Just give a small nod or a wink — snap!

Battery life is about 8–10 hours for normal use, and the glasses case doubles as a charging case. The magnetic charging means you don't even have to plug anything in. The frame design comes in trendy Boston/Wayfarer styles, so it's hard for people to tell "oh, those are smart glasses." That's the real key feature!
