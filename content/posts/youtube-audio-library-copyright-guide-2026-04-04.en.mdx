One of the biggest concerns when creating YouTube Shorts is **background music copyright**. Use the wrong track and your video could be muted, your revenue claimed by someone else, or worst case — your account penalized.

Fortunately, YouTube provides an official free music library for creators — the **YouTube Audio Library**.

---

## What Is the YouTube Audio Library?

It's a **free background music and sound effects library** built directly into YouTube Studio. You can search thousands of tracks by genre, mood, or instrument and download them for free.

![YouTube Studio Audio Library](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ef/Youtube_logo.png/320px-Youtube_logo.png)

---

## Is There a Copyright Issue?

**As long as you use the music on YouTube, there is no copyright issue.** However, each track has its own license terms, so you should always check before downloading.

```

```

### Important Notes

- **Monetization on YouTube Shorts**: Some tracks may restrict monetization in certain countries. Always review the license details before downloading.
- **External Platforms**: If you upload the same video to TikTok, Instagram Reels, etc., those platforms apply their own separate copyright policies.
- **CC-BY Tracks**: Missing the attribution credit can trigger a copyright claim.

## Step-by-Step Download Guide

### Step 1 — Go to YouTube Studio

Sign in with your Google/YouTube account and go to **YouTube Studio**.

```
URL: studio.youtube.com
```

> A Google/YouTube account is required — create one if you don't have one yet.

---

### Step 2 — Click "Audio Library"

In the left sidebar, click **Audio Library**.

```
YouTube Studio Left Menu
├── Dashboard
├── Content
├── Analytics
├── Comments
├── Subtitles
└── Audio Library  ← Click here
```

---

### Step 3 — Search and Filter Tracks

Use the filters to narrow down the mood and style you need.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│              Audio Library Filter Options                   │
├─────────────────┬───────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Filter         │  Available Options (Examples)             │
├─────────────────┼───────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Genre          │  Pop, Hip-Hop, Jazz, Electronic, etc.     │
│  Mood           │  Happy, Dark, Dramatic, Romantic, etc.    │
│  Instrument     │  Guitar, Piano, Drums, Synth, etc.        │
│  Duration       │  Under 60s → perfect for Shorts           │
│  Attribution    │  Uncheck → shows No Attribution tracks    │
└─────────────────┴───────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Shorts tip**: Set the duration filter to **under 60 seconds** to get tracks that fit Shorts length perfectly.

---

### Step 4 — Download

Once you find a track, click the **download button (↓)** on the right side of the track. It saves as an `.mp3` file.

## Summary

1. **Copyright**: Free and legal within YouTube. CC-BY tracks require attribution in the video description.
2. **Download**: YouTube Studio → Audio Library → Apply Filters → Click Download.
3. **Shorts Optimization**: Use the under-60-second duration filter.
4. **External Platforms**: Always check platform-specific policies for TikTok, Instagram, etc.

With the YouTube Audio Library, you can consistently produce high-quality Shorts without any copyright headaches.
